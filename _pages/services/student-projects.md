    <h2>Research & Technical Modelling Support</h2>

    <p>
      StrataFlux Energy provides specialist technical mentoring and modelling support for students, researchers, and early-career professionals working on subsurface energy, reservoir engineering, geochemical modelling, and computational simulation projects.
    </p>

    <p>
      Our support is designed to help you understand your modelling problem, build stronger workflows, troubleshoot simulation challenges, interpret results, and develop confidence in the technical methods behind your research.
    </p>

    <div class="ethics-box">
      <p>
        <strong>Academic integrity statement:</strong> We do not write theses, dissertations, assignments, reports, or academic submissions on behalf of students. Our role is to provide ethical technical guidance, modelling support, workflow mentoring, and research skills development.
      </p>
    </div>

    <hr>

    <h3>Who We Support</h3>

    <ul>
      <li>BSc and undergraduate students working on reservoir, energy, or geoscience projects</li>
      <li>MSc students developing simulation-based dissertations or technical research projects</li>
      <li>PhD researchers working on advanced subsurface modelling studies</li>
      <li>Postgraduate researchers needing workflow development, debugging, or model interpretation support</li>
      <li>Early-career engineers and professionals building practical modelling and simulation skills</li>
      <li>Academic groups seeking specialist support for subsurface modelling workflows</li>
    </ul>

    <hr>

    <h3>Technical Support Areas</h3>

    <ul>
      <li>Reservoir simulation workflow guidance</li>
      <li>CO₂ storage and hydrogen storage modelling support</li>
      <li>Reactive transport and geochemical modelling guidance</li>
      <li>Reservoir performance forecasting and decline analysis support</li>
      <li>Black oil and compositional simulation guidance</li>
      <li>Model setup, boundary conditions, grid design, and parameter selection</li>
      <li>Simulation troubleshooting and error interpretation</li>
      <li>Sensitivity analysis, uncertainty evaluation, and result interpretation</li>
      <li>Python-based post-processing, plotting, and automation workflows</li>
    </ul>

    <hr>

    <h3>How We Help</h3>

    <ul>
      <li>Clarifying research objectives and modelling strategy</li>
      <li>Reviewing simulation workflows and identifying technical gaps</li>
      <li>Explaining modelling assumptions, limitations, and interpretation</li>
      <li>Guiding model construction, calibration, and validation approaches</li>
      <li>Supporting data organization, result extraction, and visualization</li>
      <li>Helping you understand numerical outputs and explain results confidently</li>
      <li>Providing structured technical mentoring through one-to-one sessions</li>
    </ul>

    <hr>

    <h3>Computational & AI-Assisted Research Workflows</h3>

    <p>
      We support students and researchers in developing efficient computational workflows for simulation setup, data processing, sensitivity analysis, uncertainty evaluation, visualization, and technical interpretation.
    </p>

    <p>
      Where appropriate, AI-assisted methods can be used to improve workflow organization, automate repetitive analysis tasks, support coding productivity, screen simulation outputs, and strengthen technical understanding while maintaining academic integrity.
    </p>

    <hr>

    <h3>What This Service Is Not</h3>

    <ul>
      <li>We do not write academic work for students.</li>
      <li>We do not complete assignments, dissertations, theses, or project reports on your behalf.</li>
      <li>We do not fabricate data, results, interpretations, or references.</li>
      <li>We do not provide services that breach university academic integrity policies.</li>
    </ul>
