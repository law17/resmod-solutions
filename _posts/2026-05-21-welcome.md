Welcome! If you’re involved in carbon storage, hydrogen storage, and upstream oil & gas, you’ve come to the right place.

This blog is where we share practical, battle‑tested insights from real modelling projects. Expect:

- Deep dives into **Geologic CO₂ storage, hydrogen storage optimisation, and reservoir performance forecasting**.
- Lessons learned from history matching, uncertainty quantification, and field development planning.
- Tips for students and early‑career engineers on building simulation confidence.

Our goal is simple: to help you make reliable subsurface decisions faster. Whether you’re a reservoir engineer, a geoscientist, or a student, there will be something here for you.
